Your First Day in Island Survival

On your first day you will need to:

  • Find or create shelter
  • Get something to eat
  • Find something to drink
  • Make a campfire

A good idea once you first spawn in is to locate the shore. Here you will find a surplus of sand blocks, a valuable source of glass and metal. Every block around you in the environment will drop varying important resources.
